About Nepal

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, is a small yet extraordinary country in South Asia. It is the land of Mount Everest and Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Kathmandu
  • Population: ~30 million
  • Language: Nepali
  • Currency: Nepalese Rupee (NPR)
  • Time Zone: GMT +5:45

Geography & Nature

From the Terai plains to the Himalayas, Nepal has dramatic landscapes, rivers, glaciers, and wildlife like tigers, rhinos, and red pandas.

Culture & Heritage

A blend of Hinduism and Buddhism with unique festivals, art, and UNESCO heritage sites in the Kathmandu Valley.

Why Visit Nepal?

To stand at the world’s tallest mountains, experience diverse traditions, taste authentic food, and meet the warm Nepali people.
